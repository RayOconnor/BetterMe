BetterMe supports Google Calendar integration

To integrate you own google calendar follow the steps below...


First Make Your Calendar Public

1. In the Google Calendar interface, locate the "My calendars" area on the left.

2. Hover over the calendar you need and click the downward arrow.

3. A menu will appear. Click "Share this Calendar".

4. Check "Make this calendar public".

5. Make sure "Share only my free/busy information" is unchecked.

6. Click "Save".


Obtain Your Calendar Id And Update Your Profile

1. In the Google Calendar interface, locate the "My calendars" area on the left.

2. Hover over the calendar you need and click the downward arrow.

3. A menu will appear. Click "Calendar settings".

4. In the "Calendar Address" section of the screen, you will see your Calendar ID.

5. Update you BetterMe profile page with your Calendar Id
